About

Hi! I'm Dave Santore. In my career, my goal has always been to find a true solution vs. a surface level fix. This comes from my client facing experience in sales, account management, and customer success where defining and qualifying the appropriate metrics were necessary to providing a genuine resolution. I immediately found myself drawn towards the technical side of the clients' I've worked with and tried to learn more about software engineering and development. I liked the idea of planning, building, and maintaining the latest technology and solving today's challenges, which is why I was so thankful when I found out about General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive program.

I’ve always enjoyed learning and applying new skills to my work, but General Assembly gave me a springboard and allowed me to learn how to approach development, deconstruct problems, and to look for new ways to find a solution. They gave me the opportunity to learn different programming languages, frameworks, and other tools used in the enterprise, so I can be comfortable with them. One of the best takeaways from General Assembly I can think of is the fostering of collaboration between you and your teams. While I enjoy learning, I also enjoying the teaching and rotating group mentorship we provide to each other. Much like a real engineering job, when a group asks for help, offers advice, and researches with a purpose, any "I don’t know” feeling turns from a statement to a response ending with “…yet!”

General Assembly has given me the engineering skills and a growth mindset, and I’m looking forward to combining these with my client facing experience and solutions focused approach. I’m excited to have a stronger understanding of technical concerns, and a skillset to help address those now. With all that said, this program is not a finish line, but a strong start to a lifetime of continued learning, pushing the envelope, and challenging myself. I’m looking forward to this marathon, and am excited to find even more solutions.
